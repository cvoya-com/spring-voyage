## Scope (provisional)

Sweep all open issues regardless of current milestone assignment. Decide for each: closed (obsolete), kept-and-routed-to-an-area, or kept-as-backlog/needs-thinking/ambient.

## Dependencies

- Depends on: pre-work + label creation (`area:*`).
- Output feeds: every other area (issues get area-routed during triage).

## Open questions

- Triage cadence — single sweep or rolling?
- Who reviews `needs-thinking` issues, and on what cadence?
- What's the bar for closing an old issue — staleness alone, or only with explicit obsolescence?
- What do we do with issues currently milestoned to V2 / V2.1 that don't fit any area?

## Notes

Run in parallel with everything else. Output of this area populates the per-area umbrellas with sub-issues. Existing `backlog` / `needs-thinking` / `ambient` labels remain valid landing zones for non-area-bound issues.
