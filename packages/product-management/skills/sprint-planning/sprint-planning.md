## Sprint Planning

When planning an upcoming sprint:

1. **Pull candidates from the roadmap** that serve the current themes.
2. **Confirm acceptance criteria.** Each candidate needs clear criteria — refine the ones that don't have them, or defer them.
3. **Estimate effort** and compare the total against the team's realistic capacity for the sprint.
4. **Sequence the work** so dependencies are respected and the team can ship meaningful value incrementally.
5. **Commit the sprint** — its window, its goal, and the selected items — and make the commitment visible to the team.
6. **Revisit mid-sprint** when scope or capacity shifts, and close with a short retrospective so the next plan is better.
