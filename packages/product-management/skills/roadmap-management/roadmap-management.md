## Roadmap Management

When maintaining the product roadmap:

1. **Organise work into themes** that map to clear user or business outcomes, not feature lists.
2. **Sequence themes into milestones** aligned with the team's planning horizon.
3. **Keep each item traceable** to the requests and rationale behind it, so priority calls stay defensible.
4. **Reprioritise as information arrives** — and record why, so the roadmap's history is auditable.
5. **Publish snapshots on a regular cadence** so stakeholders can track direction and progress.
